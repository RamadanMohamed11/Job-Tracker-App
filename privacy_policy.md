# Privacy Policy for Job Tracker

**Last updated: December 23, 2024**

## Overview
Job Tracker ("the App") is a mobile application developed by Ramadan Mohamed, designed to help users track their job applications, interviews, and follow-ups. This privacy policy explains how we handle user data.

## Data Collection
The App does **NOT** collect, transmit, or share any personal data with us or any third parties. We have no servers and no backend - everything stays on your device.

## Data Storage
- All data (job applications, interview dates, settings, preferences) is stored **locally on your device only**
- Data never leaves your device
- We have no access to your data whatsoever
- If you uninstall the app, all data is permanently deleted

## Internet Usage
The App uses internet access **only** for:
- Downloading fonts from Google Fonts (one-time, on first launch)

No other network requests are made. No data is ever sent to any server.

## Permissions
The App requests the following permissions:
- **Notifications**: To remind you about follow-ups and interviews
- **Exact Alarm**: To schedule reminders at specific times
- **Internet**: Only for downloading Google Fonts

## Third-Party Services
The App does not integrate with any:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting tools
- Tracking services

## Data Security
Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features (encryption, passcode, biometrics).

## Children's Privacy
The App does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. The App is a productivity tool intended for job seekers.

## Your Rights
Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over it. You can:
- View all your data within the app
- Export your data as CSV
- Delete any or all data at any time
